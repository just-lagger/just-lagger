## Why a PM is doing this

Years of writing specs and reviewing PRs gave me strong opinions about what good products feel like, but not much hands-on time actually building them. Claude Code closes that gap enough to let me test ideas myself instead of just describing them to someone else. It's also just fun to be back in the weeds.
